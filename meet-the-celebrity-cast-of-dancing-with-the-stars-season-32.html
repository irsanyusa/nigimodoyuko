After weeks of fan speculation, Dancing With the Stars has officially confirmed of the cast members of season 32. Dancing With the Star‘s first season simultaneously streaming on Disney+ and ABC premieres on September 26. Here’s who will compete for this year’s mirrorball trophy.

Unlike past seasons, multiple celebrities were confirmed ahead of Good Morning America announcement. Vanderpump Rules star Ariana Madix was the first celebrity of the season to be announced, followed by The Bachelorette star Charity Lawson. Jamie Lynn Spears was also revealed ahead of the big cast announcement.

Meet The DWTS Season 32 Celebrities

1. Ariana Madix

Vanderpump Rules star Ariana Madix was the first of the group to be announced earlier this year. Although she was always apart of VPR, Madix’s name became nationally known because of during Tom Sandoval’s cheating scandal.

2. Charity Lawson

The Bachelorette Charity Lawson found love on the ABC dating series, and now she’s ready to waltz into the ballroom. Her involvement in the series was announced after the finale of The Bachelorette.

3. Jamie Lynn Spears

Zoey 101 and Zoey 102 star Jamie Lynn Spears was the final celebrity to be announced ahead of the full cast reveal. On Good Morning America, the actress discussed the opportunity, and how grateful she is to work amid the SAG strike.

4. Mira Sorvino

Academy Award and Golden Globe winner Mira Sorvino is known for her performance in Woody Allen’s Mighty Aphrodite, as well as The Replacement Killer, and The Final Cut.

5. Barry Williams

This actor is known as his major role of the eldest of the Brady sons, Greg Brady, on The Brady Bunch. Last season of The Masked Singer, Williams appeared alongside his TV brothers Christopher Knight and Mike Lookinland.

6. Mauricio Umansky

This reality star is known for his appearances alongside wife Kyle Richards on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. Outside of the reality series, he has a show of his own, Buying Beverly Hills.

7. Matt Walsh

Matt Walsh is an actor known for his appearances in the series “Veep.” He has also been in Ghosts, Flamin’ Hot and co-host of the podcast “Second in Command” on All Things Comedy.

8. Harry Jowsey

Reality TV star Harry Jowsey has appeared on Too Hot to Handle and Heartbreak Island.

9. Alyson Hannigan

This actress is known for her role of Lily Aldrin on the series How I Met Your Mother. She was also the host of Penn and Teller’s Fool Us.

10. Tyson Beckford

Tyson Beckford is a model known best known as a Ralph Lauren Polo model. In addition, he hosted both seasons of the Bravo show, Make Me a Supermodel.

11. Adrian Peterson

Adrian Peterson is an NFL running back. He is widely considered to be one of the greatest running backs in football history. 

12. Lele Pons

Vine, TikTok, and YouTube star Lele Pons has put down her phone, and she’s ready to take the ballroom this season.

13. Xochitl Gomez

This young actress is known for her role in the Marvel Cinematic Universe as America Chavez in “Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness. Her breakout role was in The Baby-Sitters Club.

14. Jason Mraz

“I’m Yours” and “I Won’t Give Up” hitmaker Jason Mraz is taking the ballroom this season. This feels so right since his music has been used throughout the history of the show.
