15+ Celebrity Children We Rarely See

July 2024 · 1 minute read

There are many celebrities whose children we know very well — either because they’re also famous, or because their parents post their photos on social media. A lot of us have seen Ava, the spitting image of her mom, Reese Witherspoon, or Brooklyn Beckham, who’s already made a successful career as a model and photographer. But there are also many celebrity kids that we don’t see that often.

We at Bright Side decided to dig a bit deeper and check up on some of the children of our favorite celebs that we’d like to see more of.

5. Sean and Jayden, sons of Britney Spears

7. Ruby, daughter of Tobey Maguire

8. Annie, Jamie Lee Curtis’ daughter

9. Harlow, daughter of Nicole Richie

10. Ellery and Jaya, children of Laura Dern

11. Everly, daughter of Channing Tatum

12. Aviana, daughter of Amy Adams

13. Romy, daughter of Sofia Coppola

14. Ella Bleu and Benjamin, children of John Travolta

15. Charles and Tennyson, sons of Russell Crowe

16. Rockwell, son of Lucy Liu

17. Filippa, daughter of Nikolaj Coster-Waldau
