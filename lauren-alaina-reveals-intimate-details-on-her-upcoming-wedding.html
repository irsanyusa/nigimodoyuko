Lauren Alaina Reveals Intimate Details on Her Upcoming Wedding

September 2024 · 2 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

WireImage

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Country singer Lauren Alaina recently opened up about her upcoming wedding during an interview with Audacy’s Katie Neal. The multi-talented artist, known for her powerful vocals and heartfelt songs, shared some exciting details about her wedding planning process.

Lauren Alaina is in The Planning Stages of her Upcoming Wedding

During the interview, Alaina revealed that she currently has a custom-made dress in the works for her. In addition, she mentioned that she picked out the groom, emphasizing the importance of this decision. It’s extremely clear that Alaina cannot wait to walk down the aisle with her beau.

The singer admitted that wedding planning is going well, considering she’s already secured a wedding venue. She’s also worked on other aspects of the day, but didn’t elaborate on which ones. While she didn’t go into specific details about the venue, flowers, cake, and bridesmaids’ dresses, it’s clear that she is dedicated to creating a memorable and special day.

The anticipation of seeing Alaina walk down the aisle in her dream dress adds to the excitement surrounding her upcoming nuptials.

As fans eagerly await Alaina’s new EP, Unlocked, they also have her wedding to look forward to. Her openness about the wedding planning journey allows fans to share in her happiness and eagerly await updates on this momentous occasion.

Lauren Alaina Talks About Her New EP, Collaborating Artists

Alaina’s highly anticipated EP, Unlocked, was a major highlight of the conversation. She expressed her enthusiasm about the project, emphasizing that it is a true country record. Drawing inspiration from her time spent with family, she shared how their influence shaped the songs on the EP.

The interview also highlighted Alaina’s collaboration with fellow country artist Lainey Wilson. Their duet on the EP, titled “Thicc As Thieves,” celebrates their shared experiences and camaraderie. Alaina spoke fondly of Lainey, emphasizing her work ethic and genuine personality.

During the interview, Alaina also shared a fun anecdote involving Luke Bryan. While discussing the song “Thicc As Thieves,” she revealed that they facetimed Luke to seek his blessing for sampling his hit song “Country Girl Shake It For Me” in their track.
