Looks Like Madonna: Fans Question Emily Blunts New Appearance on Oppenheimer Press Tour

July 2024 · 2 minute read

The Devil Wears Prada actress attended Oppenheimer Press Tour on July 13. People immediately noticed the change in the star’s appearance. Many compared her look to that of Madonna, which caused a big debate on social media.

Emily attended Oppenheimer press tour.

AA/ABACA/Abaca/East News, AA/ABACA/Abaca/East News

The press tour for the highly anticipated movie took place on July 13 in the bustling city of London. Among the guests were such Hollywood stars as Matt Damon, Robert Downey Jr., and Florence Pugh, who graced the event with their charismatic presence.

There was something “different” in her appearance .

Fans noticed that there was something unusual about the star’s appearance. Someone commented, “Is it just me or does Emily Blunt’s face look different?”

Others suspected plastic surgery. One user wrote, “Did Emily Blunt have some sort of surgery? Her face looks different.” Another claimed, “Just noticed the Botox on Emily Blunt’s face.”

She was compared to Madonna.

B4859 / Avalon/Photoshot/East News

It didn’t take long for people to start comparing the 40-year-old actress with the 64-year-old singer. Fans believe that Emily developed a striking similarity to Madonna. Possibly, because of the suspected plastic surgery.

Emily accepts her “imperfections”.

Scott Garfitt/Invision/East News

The star says that she embraces all her “imperfections” adding that she never felt the need to do plastic surgery.

She says, “I appreciate that every line on someone’s face carries a story. In Hollywood, people are suffocated by plastic surgery, but striving for a passive perfection isn’t something I’ve ever found beautiful or tried to achieve myself. Women who spend too much time trying to look perfect lose some of the magic about them.”

It might be twice as hard for celebs to change their looks. They are always in the limelight, and even the smallest detail can cause a lot of fuss. Madonna is a vivid example of how challenging it is to just change your style.
