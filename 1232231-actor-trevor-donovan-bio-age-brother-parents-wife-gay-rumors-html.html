How old is Trevor Donovan?

Trevor Donovan's age is 44 years as of February 2023. The actor was born on 11 October 1978. His zodiac sign is Libra.

Why is Trevor Donovan famous?

Trevor Donovan worked as a model for trending fashion brands before getting involved in the entertainment industry.

He landed his first acting role in the sitcom Quintuplets, where he played the role of a waiter. He later landed another role on the NBC soap opera Days of Our Lives as Jeremy Horton.

His breakthrough came in 2009 when he was cast to play Teddy Montgomery in the TV series 90210. He has since appeared in several films and TV series. According to his IMDb profile, he has 46 acting credits and six credits as a producer.
